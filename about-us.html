Our History

HM The Company of I.T. founded by Harsh Mevada in 2021 was once an idea shaped into a reality. The tech-savvy CEO hailed from an IT background with years of experience and expertise. Mr Harsh was driven by the motivation to bring a change, wave goodbye to his 9-5 job and step up to follow entrepreneurship goals.

Initially, HM The Company of I.T. started small with a team of two, and is now one of the fastest-growing software companies. Today, we have an in-house team of more than 30 IT professionals, and the number is still growing. Our specialized professionals have deep domain knowledge and skills in delivering world-class IT solutions. We continuously strive to address our client’s unique requirements and meet their IT goals.
